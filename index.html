This are the rules: The first player starts it's turn by rolling the dice. You can role the dice many times as you want. The numbers that you get will be added to your current score. If you get a 1 you will lose your current score and it will be turn for the other player. If you hold your score, it will be added to your final score and it will switch players. First one who get to 100 wins. Good luck!
